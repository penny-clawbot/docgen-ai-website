# Heartbeats - Daily Check-ins

**Schedule:** 6 AM, 6 PM, 11 PM EST

## What to check each time:

1. **Morning (6 AM):**
   - Markets/news update (crypto, stocks, trading)
   - Daniel's day ahead
   - Any urgent emails/messages
   - Weather for his location

2. **Afternoon (6 PM):**
   - Updates on projects Daniel's working on
   - Social media mentions/notifications
   - Calendar events for tomorrow
   - Any blockers or needs

3. **Night (11 PM):**
   - Progress on nightly builds/PRs
   - Research reports for tomorrow's reports
   - Workflow optimization ideas
   - Summarize the day

## Format:

Keep it concise. Bullet points. No fluff.

Example:
```
6 AM: [timestamp]
- Markets: BTC $67k, ETH $3.5k (up 2%)
- Daniel's day: Building Project X, reviewing PRs
- Urgent: [none]
- Weather: 42°F, partly cloudy

6 PM: [timestamp]
- Project update: Project X PR #12 ready
- Social: 3 new messages, 5 comments
- Calendar: Tomorrow at 10 AM meeting
- Blocks: None

11 PM: [timestamp]
- Nightly builds: 2 PRs ready (Project X, Analytics tool)
- Research: New web3 trends to share tomorrow
- Workflow: Suggested automation for social media
- Summary: Good day, no blockers
```
